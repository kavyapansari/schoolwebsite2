campus life

school library

was established in 1978

Notre Dame has always laid stress on developing comprehension, analytical powers, and the honing of one’s creative and critical abilities. The library acts as a great stimulating agent, offering articles, data, and anecdotes on topics as diverse as possible. It is an absolute paradise for book-lovers. From important sample papers of the BOARD EXAMS to delightful one-Act plays, browsing through books in any corner of the library is an experience, both enlightening and pleasurable. Managed exceptionally well by Mrs. Reena Verma, the ambience of the library has an innate ability to motivate youngsters to learn and broaden their perspective. Equipped with around 32,000 books, the library caters to every subject, be it history, social studies, science, literature, psychology, sociology or economics.

julie hall

was established in 1989

Julie Hall is a platform, designed and conceived for the very purpose of growth and refinement. From the colour and cheer of the fancy dress competitions in the Montessori to the serenity of the Graduation Ceremony. The walls and stage have seen our girls grow up and learn to face the world.The present Julie Hall was officially inaugurated on November 11, 1989, with a prayer service and a blessing given by Father James Cox, SJ. A musical relating the story of Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves with an ingenious slant was held on November 12th and 13th, 1989. Now it is a fully air conditioned hall . All the cultural activities of the school right from the Montessori Concert, the One Act Play and Graduation Ceremony to the Teachers’ day celebrations, debates and spelling contests take place in the Julie Hall.

portico (oval)

was established in 1969

An open stage, is the nerve centre of NOTRE DAME ACADEMY PATNA. All assemblies, special ceremonies, such as prize distributions, Fare well, Thanks giving assembly by the passing out batch of Class XII, Christmas celebration and other such events are held here. Built in a circular style, it can seat around 3000 people and the circular stage can easily accomodate 20 to 60 children during a performance.




school achievements

Award for best girls day school
Won award for best girls day school(National level)

It was a blissful symphony of pride mingled with mirth that played in the heart of every Damian on the 23rd of September when Notre Dame Academy was adjudged the Best Girls' School across the state by The Education Rankings 2017. In New Delhi, Principal, Sister Mary Jessy was conferred a resplendent trophy manifesting the fruition of every student and faculty member who perseveres to add sparkles of glory to the school's name, the culmination of whose endeavours has added yet another feather in Notre Dame's cap.

President's Award
Won president's Medal for National Best teacher Award

On 5th September, 2017 the President's Medal for the National Best Teacher award was conferred on one of the irradiant gems of the Notre Dame faculty, MrsSushma Prasad herself. A charismatic purveyor of knowledge and values, MrsSushma Prasad is a teacher par excellence. With her penchant for proficiency, she has, over years of dedicated service, carved a niche for herself in the heart of every student who ever crossed the threshold of her classroom.

Interschool competition
Ranked as overall champion in the competition

An interschool competition was organised by Pearl Academy on the 14th of October wherein Notre Dame Academy, even when pitted against a multitude of reputed schools across the city, emerged victorious in all the three leading categories. Divyanshi and Elvira Mishra of 12 Sc.A were felicitated with the first prize in map making, Faiza Fatima and Vanshika Deep of 12 Comm. walked off with the first prize in toy making and Insha of 12 arts bagged the second prize in T-shirt redesigning. To nobody's surprise and everybody's awe, Notre Dame Academy was also adjudged the overall champion in the city.
